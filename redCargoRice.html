Red Cargo Rice

Red cargo rice is a type of unpolished whole-grain rice with a reddish-brown outer husk, popular in Southeast Asian cuisines. Its distinctive color comes from anthocyanins, natural pigments rich in antioxidants. Unlike white rice, red cargo rice retains its bran layer, making it more nutritious and higher in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It has a nutty flavor and a slightly chewy texture when cooked, making it a wholesome choice for health-conscious meals.

This rice variety is versatile and pairs well with both savory and sweet dishes. Commonly used in rice bowls, salads, and side dishes, red cargo rice absorbs flavors beautifully while adding a hearty texture. Its nutrient density and rich taste make it an increasingly popular alternative to refined rice varieties in modern cooking.
